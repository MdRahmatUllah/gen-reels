# Phase 6 Overview: Collaboration And Studio

## Objective

Expand the product from solo creator workflow into a team-ready platform for agencies and internal brand teams.

## Why This Phase Exists

Studio and agency value comes from shared workflows, approvals, brand consistency, and accountability. These needs should only arrive after the core creator pipeline is reliable and reusable.

## In Scope

- Workspace roles and permissions expansion
- Shared templates and presets
- Brand kits
- Comments and approvals
- Webhook delivery for workspace events
- Workspace API keys for automation and webhook verification
- Audit trails
- Team-level usage and project visibility

## Out Of Scope

- Local execution
- Deep on-prem enterprise packaging

## What Users Get

- Team collaboration in a shared workspace
- Brand consistency controls
- Approval and review flows
- Visibility into who changed what and when

## Deliverables

- Role model expansion
- Brand kit service
- Approval workflow enhancements
- Commenting and review surfaces
- Webhook configuration and delivery
- Workspace API key lifecycle management
- Audit trail records

## Exit Criteria

- A team can collaborate on the same project without confusion over ownership or history.
- Agencies can reuse approved presets and templates across multiple projects.
- Brand-critical settings can be enforced at the workspace level.
